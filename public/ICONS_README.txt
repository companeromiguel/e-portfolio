FAVICON AND PWA ICONS SETUP
===========================

To complete your portfolio setup, you need to add the following icon files to this /public folder:

1. favicon.ico (16x16 or 32x32 pixels)
   - This appears in browser tabs
   - You can create one at: https://favicon.io/

2. icon-192.png (192x192 pixels)
   - Used for PWA on mobile devices
   - Should be a square PNG with your logo/initials

3. icon-512.png (512x512 pixels)
   - Used for PWA on larger displays
   - Should be a square PNG with your logo/initials

QUICK SETUP:
1. Go to https://realfavicongenerator.net/
2. Upload your logo or profile picture
3. Download the generated package
4. Copy the files to this /public folder

Your icons should match your green portfolio theme for consistency!
